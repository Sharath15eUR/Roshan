Process:
1. A process is an instance of a program that is being executed or processed.
2. A process executes a particular task.
3. Processes are independent of each other and hence don't share a memory or other resources.

Thread:
1. Thread is a segment of a process or a lightweight process that is managed by the scheduler independently.
2. A single or multiple thread(s) together execute a process.
3. Threads are interdependent and share memory and other resources.
